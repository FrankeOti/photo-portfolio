About

I am a photographer driven by a deep passion for capturing authentic moments and telling stories through images. My work is rooted in simplicity, emotion, and the natural beauty of light. Through my work, I hope to create images that not only capture what is seen, but also what is felt. Based in Nairobi, I draw inspiration from everyday life, culture, and the subtle details that often go unnoticed.
